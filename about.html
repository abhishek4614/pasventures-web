About Us

PAS Ventures is the parent company of Bizfix and Haye. Under the Umbrella of PAS Ventures, Bizfix and Haye provide their clients a large variety of services and products. From business solutions for upliftment of our client’s companies to bike riding services for our clients, PAS ventures provide you all. We and our team of experts work hard for strengthening the global presence of our client’s companies and for easing the lives of our customers. PAS Ventures is based in India but it is successfully operating all around the globe. Our clients are not only restricted to our nation but we provide IT services internationally.
